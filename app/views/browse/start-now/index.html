Get support for your long term health condition or disability

This service helps you find benefits, support, and resources tailored to your long-term health condition.

To give you the most accurate recommendations, we will need to share some of your information:

What we’ll use:

  • Your medical information from the NHS
  • Benefits information from the Department for Work and Pensions

You will be asked to consent to share your data once you sign in.

Before you start

You’ll need a GOV.UK One Login to use this service. If you do not have a GOV.UK One Login, you can create one.
