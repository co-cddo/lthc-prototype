Other benefits and financial help

If you have a health condition that affects your work, you may be able to apply for other benefits apart from Universal Credit.

Use a benefits calculator to see which benefits you could get and how much you might receive each week.

You might also be able to get other financial help with health costs, bills and travel.

Health costs

Get help with NHS prescriptions, dental treatment, eye tests and other costs.

Bills

Get help with the cost of living if you have a health condition or disability.

For example you might be able to get:

  • your water bill capped through the Watersure scheme
  • £150 off energy bills with the Warm Home Discount Scheme
  • a discount on your broadband bills, if you’re claiming benefits

Travel

You may get help with transport costs if you have a disability.
