Benefits and financial help

Getting PIP may mean you can get extra help with:

  • Housing Benefit
  • Council Tax reduction
  • Universal Credit
  • Carer’s Allowance (if someone looks after you)
  • Blue Badge for parking
  • The Motability scheme (to lease a car, scooter or powered wheelchair)

You can check what benefits you could get using an independent benefits calculator.
