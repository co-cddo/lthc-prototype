What happens next

Your application for a Disabled Facilities Grant is being reviewed by your local council. They will assess whether you qualify for funding to make changes to your home that support your disability.

The council may contact you if they need more information before making a decision.

What this means for you

A decision is expected by 15 June 2025. If more information is needed, the council will contact you. If your grant is approved, it will help cover the cost of the agreed home adaptations.

You do not need to do anything right now, but you can contact your local council if you have any questions.
