Get support from your employer

Your employer must make any reasonable adjustments so that you’re not disadvantaged at work.

{{ govukInsetText({ html: 'Access to Work grants do not pay for reasonable adjustments, as it’s your employer’s responsibility to make these changes.' }) }}

You can use the Health Adjustment Passport form when talking with your employer. It helps you work out any reasonable adjustments you need.

For example, you may need:

  • flexible or part-time working
  • to speak to an occupational health adviser in your organisation
  • time off for health appointments

If you’re having problems with your employer

You can get advice and help from various organisations.

If you’re in a trade union, they can represent you when talking to your employer.

You can also contact the following for free advice:

  • ACAS
  • Citizens Advice
